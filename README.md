## 📄 **Content Ownership & Licensing Model**

### **🏢 Ownership Structure**
Route 4 Studios, LLC retains **full copyright and ownership** of all produced content. This unique model ensures:
- **Quality Control**: Maintaining our high production standards across all content
- **Strategic Distribution**: Maximizing content reach and audience engagement
- **Long-term Value**: Building a comprehensive content library for sustained impact

### **📋 Licensing Framework**

#### **Phase 1: Exclusive Distribution License (3 Months)**
- **Duration**: 3 months from delivery date
- **Client Benefits**: 
  - Exclusive usage rights for immediate marketing and distribution needs
  - **80/20 revenue split** (client receives 80% of VOD revenue)
  - Exclusive VOD service on our streaming platform
  - Live streaming capabilities with platform URI integration (upcharge available)

#### **Phase 2: Public Distribution (Post 3-Month Period)**
- **Expanded Reach**: Content becomes publicly available on Route 4 Studios streaming platforms
- **Maximum Visibility**: Broader audience reach and enhanced discoverability
- **Continued Revenue**: Ongoing revenue sharing from expanded distribution

### **📊 Revenue Distribution Schedule**
```
Months 1-3: Client (80%) | Route 4 Studios (20%)
Month 4+:   Public distribution with continued revenue sharing
```
